About Us

Welcome To Thunder Cutz Barbershop

We continue to learn from each other within a friendly atmosphere to provide you with a professional enjoyable barber service. During our Vidal Sassoon training we studied decade related fashion trends, the 20’s, 30’s, 40’s, 50’s and so on.

Each decade culminated in a professional choreographed fashion show, it’s this learning that we put into every single haircut. Today’s trends are always historically influenced but with a new twist, we do that but with 25 years experience we’re also able to add our own style if needed.
